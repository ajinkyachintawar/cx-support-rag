# Escalation rules

Policy code: ESC-002

Cases must be escalated to a senior agent or supervisor when any of the
following apply:

- Customer explicitly requests a supervisor
- Refund request falls outside policy window due to a service failure on
  our end (late delivery, system outage, billing error)
- Customer has had 2 or more unresolved contacts on the same issue
- Any mention of legal action, regulatory complaint, or media contact
- Data privacy concern (see DPR-001) of any kind, escalate immediately,
  do not attempt to resolve directly

Escalation SLA: senior agents must respond within 4 business hours. Use the
internal ticketing system, tag the ticket "ESCALATION" and select the
appropriate reason code from the dropdown.

Do not promise a specific resolution outcome to the customer when escalating
— only confirm that the case is being reviewed by a senior team member.
