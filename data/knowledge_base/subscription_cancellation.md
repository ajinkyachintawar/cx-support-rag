# Subscription cancellation procedure

Policy code: SUB-009

When a customer requests to cancel their premium subscription:

1. Confirm they understand cancellation takes effect at the end of the
   current billing cycle, not immediately — they retain premium benefits
   until then
2. No partial refund is issued for the remaining days in the current cycle,
   regardless of how early in the cycle the cancellation request is made
3. If the customer demands an immediate refund for unused days, this is
   not standard policy — escalate only if the customer cites a specific
   service failure as the cancellation reason (e.g. "I'm cancelling because
   my last 3 orders were all delayed"), otherwise politely decline and
   confirm the end-of-cycle cancellation
4. Cancellation does not retroactively affect refund windows for items
   already purchased while on premium tier — see CASE-0163 for the
   relevant precedent

This procedure is separate from refund policy (REF-PREM-030) — cancellation
is about the subscription itself, refund policy is about individual item
purchases.
