# Refund policy — standard tier customers

Policy code: REF-STD-014

Standard tier customers are eligible for a full refund if the request is
made within 14 calendar days of the original purchase date. Refunds are
processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

If the 14-day window has passed, the customer is not eligible for a refund
under standard policy. They may be offered store credit at the agent's
discretion if the case involves a service failure on our end (see escalation
rules, document ESC-002).

Refund requests after 14 days for reasons other than service failure should
be politely declined, with the option to escalate to a supervisor if the
customer insists.
