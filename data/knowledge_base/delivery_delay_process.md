# Delivery delay process

Policy code: DEL-007

If a customer reports a delivery is delayed beyond the original estimated
delivery date:

1. Check tracking status in the internal logistics dashboard first
2. If delay is under 3 days past estimate, inform customer of new estimate,
   no compensation required
3. If delay is 3-7 days past estimate, offer a $10 service credit
4. If delay exceeds 7 days, offer a full refund OR free reshipment,
   customer's choice, and this counts as a service failure under ESC-002,
   meaning the standard 14-day refund window does not apply
5. If the item shows as "delivered" in tracking but customer says they
   never received it, this is treated as a lost package, not a delay —
   follow lost package process (document LOG-011) instead

Always apologize for the inconvenience before explaining the resolution.
Do not blame the courier by name.
