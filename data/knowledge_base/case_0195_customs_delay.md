# Resolved case — customs delay on international order

Case ID: CASE-0195

Situation: International customer's order was held at customs for 12 days,
well past the standard delivery delay thresholds in DEL-007, which assumes
domestic shipping.

Resolution: Customs holds are explicitly NOT covered by the standard
delivery delay process (DEL-007), since that process assumes domestic
courier delays within our control. Customs delays are outside our control
and do not count as a service failure under ESC-002. The customer was
informed that customs delays cannot be expedited by us and no compensation
applies, but offered a tracking link update and estimated customs clearance
time based on the destination country's typical processing time.

Note for future agents: check the shipping address country before applying
DEL-007 — if international, customs hold time does not count toward the
3-day or 7-day compensation thresholds. This is a known gap in current
documentation and was flagged to the policy team for a dedicated
international shipping SOP.
