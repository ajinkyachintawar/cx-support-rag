# Complaint handling SOP

Policy code: SOP-COMP-005

General complaints (not tied to a specific refund, delivery, or privacy
issue — e.g. complaints about product quality, website usability, or
customer service experience from a previous interaction) follow this
process:

1. Acknowledge the complaint specifically, do not give a generic apology
2. Log the complaint in the feedback system with category tag, this is
   separate from the support ticket system
3. If the complaint is about a previous agent's conduct, do not comment on
   that agent's performance to the customer, simply log it and route to
   team lead review
4. Offer a resolution only if one is reasonably available (e.g. product
   quality complaint with no return request yet — offer the standard
   refund process information)
5. If no resolution is available or appropriate, the complaint can be
   logged for feedback purposes without a compensatory offer — not every
   complaint requires compensation

This SOP does not apply to billing errors (see CASE-0142 pattern), delivery
issues (DEL-007), or privacy requests (DPR-001) — those have their own
dedicated processes and should not be routed through general complaint
logging.
