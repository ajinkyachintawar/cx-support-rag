# Refund policy — premium tier customers

Policy code: REF-PREM-030

Premium tier customers (identified by account flag PREM_ACTIVE) are eligible
for a full refund within 30 calendar days of purchase, double the standard
tier window. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days, faster than
standard tier due to priority processing queue.

Premium customers requesting a refund between day 30 and day 45 may be
offered partial store credit (50% of purchase value) at agent discretion.
After day 45, no refund or credit applies regardless of tier.

Do not confuse this with standard tier policy (REF-STD-014), which has a
14-day window. Always check the customer's tier flag before quoting a
refund window.
