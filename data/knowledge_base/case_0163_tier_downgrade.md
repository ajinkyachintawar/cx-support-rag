# Resolved case — customer downgraded from premium to standard before refund request

Case ID: CASE-0163

Situation: Customer purchased an item while on premium tier (30-day refund
window applies under REF-PREM-030), then cancelled their premium
subscription 10 days later, then requested a refund on day 20 — after
losing premium status but within what would have been the premium window.

Resolution: The refund window is determined by the customer's tier status
AT THE TIME OF PURCHASE, not at the time of the refund request. Since the
customer was premium when they bought the item, the 30-day window applied
and the refund was approved on day 20. Tier changes after purchase do not
retroactively shorten an already-active refund window.

Note for future agents: always check tier status at purchase date in order
history, not current account status, when determining which refund policy
applies.
